Hello, JPCS-UE Member! 👋

We are excited to share that JPCS-UE Manila Chapter has been invited to participate in the JPCS Leadership Transformation Workshop and Election (LTWE) 2025, a national leadership event organized by the Philippine Computer Society (PCS) and JPCS National!

📅 Event Details

  • Event: JPCS Leadership Transformation Workshop & Election 2025
  • Theme: "Synergy in Action: Empowering Tomorrow's Tech Leaders Today"
  • Date: December 9, 2025 (Tuesday), 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM
  • Platform: Zoom (Online), streamed on Discord

This event aims to build leadership skills, teamwork, and collaboration among JPCS officers and members nationwide. Participants can look forward to inspiring talks, interactive activities, and leadership-building sessions, culminating in the election, announcement, and oath-taking of the JPCS National Board of Officers for AY 2025–2026.

📢 Important Announcement

Due to limited slots, only JPCS-UE Officers and our Chapter Adviser will be attending the official Zoom session. However, all interested members are welcome to watch the livestream on our official Discord server!

This is also a great opportunity for you to join our official Discord server and Messenger group chat so you can stay up to date with the latest happenings, announcements, and events from JPCS-UE! 🚀
