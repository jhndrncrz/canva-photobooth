JPCS Photo Booth

Canva App for Event Photography

About This App

JPCS Photo Booth is a Canva application that brings the fun of photo booth experiences directly into your Canva designs. Perfect for events, parties, and creative projects!

Features

Easy Capture

Take photos directly from any device with a camera

Auto Mode

Automated photo capture with countdown timer

Frame Detection

Automatically detects photo frames in your template

Mobile Friendly

Use your phone as a remote camera

Camera Controls

Mirror, grid overlay, and camera switching

Retake Support

Easily retake individual or all photos

About the Developer

Junior Philippine Computer Society

UE Manila Chapter

We are a student organization dedicated to promoting computer science and technology education. This app was developed to provide a fun, interactive tool for capturing memories at our events.
