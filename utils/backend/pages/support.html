Frequently Asked Questions

Why can't the app access my camera?

Make sure you've granted camera permissions in your browser. Click the camera/lock icon in your browser's address bar to check permissions. Some browsers require HTTPS for camera access.

The camera link isn't working. What should I do?

Camera links expire after 30 minutes. Try generating a new link by clicking "Copy Camera Link" again in the Canva app. Make sure you're using the complete URL.

My photos aren't appearing in the design.

Ensure you clicked "Submit All Photos" after capturing. The photos should automatically appear in the designated frame positions. If not, try refreshing the Canva editor.

Can I switch between front and back cameras?

Yes! Click the "Switch" button in the camera toolbar to toggle between available cameras on your device.

Does this work on mobile devices?

Yes, the camera page works on mobile devices with modern browsers. Open the camera link on your phone to capture photos using your mobile camera.

Troubleshooting

1

Clear Browser Cache

Sometimes cached data can cause issues. Try clearing your browser cache or using an incognito/private window.

2

Check Camera Permissions

Go to your browser settings and ensure camera access is allowed for the camera page domain.

3

Try a Different Browser

If issues persist, try using Chrome, Firefox, or Edge. Safari may have additional permission requirements.

4

Generate a New Session

If the session expired, go back to the Canva app and start a new capture session.

5

Check Your Connection

Ensure you have a stable internet connection for photo transfer between the camera page and Canva.

Report a Bug

Found a bug or have a feature request? Please report it on our GitHub repository:

  • Visit github.com/jhndrncrz/canva-photobooth/issues
  • Click "New Issue"
  • Include steps to reproduce the problem
  • Describe what you expected vs what happened
  • Include browser and device information if relevant

Response Times

We're a student organization, so response times may vary. Generally:

  • GitHub Issues: Within 1-3 days
  • Email: Within 3-5 business days

For urgent issues, please create a GitHub issue with the "urgent" label.
