Privacy Policy

JPCS Photo Booth - Canva App

Effective Date: December 9, 2025

Introduction

The Junior Philippine Computer Society - UE Manila Chapter ("JPCS", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use the JPCS Photo Booth application ("the App").

Summary: We collect minimal data, process photos temporarily, and do not sell or share your information with third parties.

Information We Collect

Camera Access

The App requires access to your device's camera to capture photos. This access is requested through your browser's permission system and can be revoked at any time through your browser settings.

Photo Data

When you use the App, photos are temporarily processed through our servers to transfer them to your Canva design. We do not:

  • Permanently store your photos
  • Use your photos for any purpose other than the immediate transfer to Canva
  • Share your photos with third parties
  • Train AI models using your photos

Session Data

We temporarily store the following information during your session:

Data Type Purpose Retention
Session ID Link camera page to Canva app 30 minutes
Photo data (base64) Transfer photos to Canva Until session ends
Capture settings Configure photo capture Until session ends

Information We Do NOT Collect

We explicitly do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, address)
  • Device identifiers or fingerprints
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Cookies for advertising purposes

How We Use Information

The information we process is used solely to:

  • Facilitate photo capture and transfer to your Canva design
  • Maintain active sessions during use
  • Provide the core functionality of the App

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transfers
  • Automatic session cleanup after 30 minutes of inactivity
  • In-memory storage only (no persistent database)
  • No logging of photo content

Third-Party Services

The App operates within the Canva platform. When you use the App:

  • Canva may collect data according to their own privacy policy
  • Photos are transferred to Canva's platform for insertion into your design
  • We recommend reviewing Canva's Privacy Policy

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Deny camera access at any time through your browser
  • Close your session to immediately remove all temporary data
  • Request information about data processing
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
