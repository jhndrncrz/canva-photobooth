2. Description of Service

JPCS Photo Booth is a Canva application that allows users to:

  • Capture photos using their device's camera
  • Automatically place captured photos into Canva design templates
  • Configure capture settings such as countdown timer and number of photos

The App operates within the Canva platform and requires an active Canva account to use.

3. User Requirements
